Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is YelloPago?

YelloPago is a platform that connects homeowners and businesses with verified service providers. We make it easy to find, book, and pay for professional services.

How do I get started?

Simply create an account as either a client or service provider. Once registered, you can start booking services or listing your professional services.

For Clients

How do I book a service?

Search for the service you need, compare providers, and book directly through our platform. You can view provider availability and make secure payments online.

Are services guaranteed?

Yes, all services booked through YelloPago are backed by our satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll work to make it right.

For Service Providers

How do I join as a provider?

Register as a service provider, complete your profile, and undergo our verification process. Once approved, you can start receiving booking requests.

What are the fees?

We charge a small commission on completed bookings. View our pricing page for detailed information about our fee structure.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any questions you might have.
